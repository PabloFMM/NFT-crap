The Game

GM, traveller. The city of Veel-Tark opens its doors to you, inviting you to join our community to contribute to the repopulation of the place. A decentralised city-state, without limits or borders, rescued from a utopian fantasy and reborn from imagination.

Get ready, because the community of Veel-Tark is gathered around a game of riddles, quests and challenges. A role-playing game born in the virtual world to be projected into real life. Puzzles, escape rooms and conundrums await you on the other side.

Enter the gate and play the first adventure

One of these gates will take you to Veel-Tark. The other two will lead you into a bottomless abyss. The problem is that two of the gates always lie, and only one always tells the truth.

Yellow: The red gate will not take you to Veel-Tark. I will take you to Veel-Tark.

Red: The blue gate will not take you to Veel-Tark. I will take you to Veel-Tark.

Blue: I will take you to Veel-Tark.
